Hustle Pool — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data to provide and improve our game:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifier
  • Gameplay Data: Match results, scores, in-game progress
  • Purchase Data: In-app purchase history (processed by Google Play / Apple)
  • Analytics: Session duration, feature usage, crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide matchmaking and leaderboard services
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent cheating and enforce fair play

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • PlayFab (Microsoft): Account management, matchmaking, leaderboards
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google): Crash reporting
  • Unity Analytics: Game analytics
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: Payment processing

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Hustle Pool is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.
